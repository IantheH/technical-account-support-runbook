# Post-Resolution Follow-Up Template

Use this template when closing the loop with a customer after a technical issue has been resolved.

The goal is to confirm the issue is fixed, explain what changed in customer-friendly language, recommend next steps, and identify any follow-up documentation or process improvements.

## Confirmation Checklist

Before closing the issue, confirm:

- [ ] Customer understands what changed
- [ ] Customer has tested the workflow
- [ ] Customer confirmed the issue is resolved
- [ ] Any workaround is documented
- [ ] Internal notes are updated
- [ ] Follow-up training or documentation needs are captured
- [ ] Account health impact is noted, if relevant

---

## Post-Resolution Review

After an issue is resolved, ask:

- What caused the issue?
- Could it happen again?
- Was the customer communication clear?
- Did the issue reveal a training or documentation gap?
- Should a workflow, automation, or support article be updated?
- Is there a process improvement opportunity?
- Should this be mentioned in a future account review or QBR?

---

## Customer Success Impact

Technical support is part of Customer Success because every issue affects trust.

A strong resolution process can improve:

- customer confidence
- product adoption
- account health
- stakeholder trust
- renewal readiness
- customer self-sufficiency
- internal team alignment

The goal is not only to fix the issue. The goal is to help the customer continue moving forward with confidence.
